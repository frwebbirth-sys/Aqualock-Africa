Most Popular
PRODUCT 01

LM Berea Lawn Grass

LM Berea is South Africa's most beloved lawn grass — and for good reason. This soft, dense, shade-tolerant variety thrives in the Western Cape's mild coastal climate, producing a lush, deep-green carpet that stays verdant year-round. Its medium blade width and fine texture make it ideal for residential gardens, school grounds, and low-traffic recreational areas. Requiring moderate watering and maintenance, LM Berea offers an excellent balance between beauty and practicality for homeowners who want a premium lawn without excessive upkeep.

High Performance
PRODUCT 02

Kikuyu Turf Grass

Kikuyu is the workhorse of South African turf — a vigorous, fast-growing grass renowned for its exceptional durability and rapid self-repair. Ideal for high-traffic areas such as sports fields, golf course roughs, and active family gardens, Kikuyu establishes quickly and recovers from wear with remarkable speed. Its coarse, bright-green blades handle full sun exposure superbly and demonstrate impressive drought resistance once established. Chosen by groundskeepers at stadiums, schools, and municipal parks across the Western Cape, Kikuyu is the trusted choice where performance and resilience are non-negotiable.

Sports Grade
PRODUCT 03

Bermuda / Cynodon Turf

Bermuda grass (Cynodon dactylon) is the international gold standard for professional sports surfaces — used on cricket pitches, football fields, tennis courts, and golf fairways worldwide. Its incredibly fine, dense blade structure creates a tight, consistent playing surface that rolls true and withstands the most intense wear. Bermuda's deep root system gives it outstanding heat and drought tolerance, while its capacity for extremely close mowing makes it the preferred choice for surfaces that demand precision. When professional standards are required, Bermuda grass is the definitive solution.

Water Wise
PRODUCT 04

Buffalo Grass

Buffalo grass is nature's answer to the challenge of maintaining a beautiful lawn under water-scarce conditions. With one of the highest drought tolerances of any warm-season turfgrass, Buffalo thrives in South Africa's increasingly water-conscious landscaping environment. Its wide, soft blades form a dense mat that suppresses weeds naturally and remains attractively green even through extended dry spells with minimal irrigation. Particularly well-suited to coastal gardens, retirement estates, and commercial landscaping projects where water efficiency and low maintenance costs are priorities, Buffalo grass delivers lasting beauty without compromise.
